# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

ATLAS-GATE-MCP is committed to creating a welcoming, inclusive, and professional community. We value contributions from everyoneâ€”regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

This Code of Conduct outlines expectations for all community members and the consequences for unacceptable behavior.

---

## Our Values

### **Respect**
- Treat all community members with dignity and courtesy
- Welcome diverse perspectives and experiences
- Listen actively and assume good intent

### **Inclusivity**
- Create a welcoming environment for people of all backgrounds
- Actively work to eliminate barriers to participation
- Amplify underrepresented voices

### **Collaboration**
- Work together toward common goals
- Offer constructive feedback and support
- Celebrate each other's contributions

### **Professionalism**
- Maintain professional standards in all interactions
- Focus on ideas, not on individuals
- Resolve conflicts constructively

### **Integrity**
- Be honest and transparent
- Follow through on commitments
- Take responsibility for mistakes

---

## Expected Behavior

Community members are expected to:

1. **Be Respectful**
   - Use inclusive language
   - Respect differing opinions and experiences
   - Accept constructive criticism gracefully

2. **Be Collaborative**
   - Share knowledge and help others
   - Give credit to others' contributions
   - Work toward consensus where possible

3. **Be Professional**
   - Keep discussions focused on the topic
   - Avoid personal attacks or ad hominem arguments
   - Respect confidentiality when appropriate

4. **Be Considerate**
   - Be mindful of how your words affect others
   - Respect others' time and boundaries
   - Seek to understand before being understood

5. **Be Honest**
   - Disclose conflicts of interest
   - Report issues in good faith
   - Correct mistakes openly

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated in this community:

### **Harassment**
- Unwelcome comments or conduct based on protected characteristics (race, ethnicity, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, age, religion, or other status)
- Repeated unwelcome contact after being asked to stop
- Threats or intimidation
- Stalking or doxxing
- Unwelcome physical contact

### **Discrimination**
- Exclusion based on protected characteristics
- Denial of opportunities based on identity
- Stereotyping or prejudicial comments

### **Hostile Conduct**
- Deliberate intimidation
- Aggressive or abusive language
- Personal attacks or insults
- Dismissing others' contributions or expertise

### **Misconduct**
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Fraud or deception
- Plagiarism or credit theft
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior

### **Professional Violations**
- Spam or unsolicited commercial content
- Solicitation without proper disclosure
- Copyright or trademark violations
- Attempts to manipulate project processes

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- **GitHub Issues and Discussions**: All interactions
- **Pull Requests**: Comments and review discussions
- **Email Communication**: Official project communications
- **Community Events**: Meetups, conferences, or community gatherings
- **Public Statements**: Representing the project publicly

This Code of Conduct does not apply to:

- Private conversations between community members
- Content not directly related to the project
- Off-topic discussions in appropriate forums

---

## Reporting Violations

### **How to Report**

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **For Non-Emergency Issues**:
   - Email: conduct@atlas-gate-mcp.org
   - Include: Description of incident, people involved, witnesses, links to relevant content

2. **For Urgent/Threatening Issues**:
   - Email: emergency@atlas-gate-mcp.org
   - Response time: Within 24 hours
   - Include: Safety concerns and immediate needs

3. **For Private Matters**:
   - Email: conduct@atlas-gate-mcp.org with subject "CONFIDENTIAL"
   - Your privacy will be respected

### **What to Include in Report**

Help us understand the situation:
- What happened and when
- Who was involved (pseudonyms are OK)
- How it affected you or the community
- Any witnesses or evidence
- What you'd like to happen

**Note**: You don't need to be the target of unacceptable behavior to report it. If you witness something concerning, please report it.

---

## Investigation Process

### **What We'll Do**

1. **Acknowledge**: Receipt acknowledged within 48 hours
2. **Investigate**: Impartial, confidential investigation
3. **Consult**: Talk to relevant people and gather facts
4. **Decide**: Determine if Code of Conduct was violated
5. **Communicate**: Inform reporter and offender of outcome
6. **Document**: Record for pattern-detection

### **Timeline**

- **Simple cases**: Resolution within 7 days
- **Complex cases**: Resolution within 14 days
- **Extended cases**: Updates every 7 days

### **Confidentiality**

- Reporter identity protected (unless they prefer otherwise)
- Investigation details kept confidential
- Only necessary parties informed of outcome
- Results documented securely

---

## Consequences

Consequences are proportional to the severity and pattern of behavior:

### **First Violation (Minor)**
- Private conversation to clarify expectations
- Warning issued

### **Second Violation or First (Moderate)**
- Temporary removal (3-7 days)
- Requirements for re-entry (e.g., acknowledgment, training)

### **Severe First Violation or Third Violation**
- Permanent removal from community
- May be reported to law enforcement if necessary

### **Pattern of Violations**
- Immediate escalation to consequences
- May include legal action if appropriate

**Note**: Serious violations (threats, doxxing, harassment) may result in immediate permanent removal without warning.

---

## Appeals Process

If you disagree with the outcome:

1. **Request Appeal**: Email conduct@atlas-gate-mcp.org within 7 days
2. **Provide Context**: Explain why you believe the decision was unfair
3. **Independent Review**: A different team reviews the case
4. **Final Decision**: Made within 14 days

Appeals are taken seriously and reviewed impartially.

---

## Enforcement

### **Who Enforces This**

- **Code of Conduct Committee**: Appointed group responsible for enforcement
- **Project Maintainers**: Can remove or restrict access to community spaces
- **Community Moderators**: Can delete inappropriate content and issue warnings

### **Authority**

The Code of Conduct Committee has final authority over interpretation and enforcement. Decisions can be appealed through the process above.
